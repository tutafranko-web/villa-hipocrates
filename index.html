About the Villa

A Private Sanctuary
on Hvar Island

Nestled in the tranquil hills above Vrboska, just 900 metres from the crystalline waters of Oskorušica cove, Villa Harpocrates is Hvar at its most intimate and unhurried. A five-bedroom estate of uncompromising elegance, crafted for those who demand privacy without sacrificing pleasure.

From the temperature-controlled pool that shimmers under the Dalmatian sun, to the candlelit wine cellar stocked with Croatian and international vintages — every detail has been considered. Twelve mountain bikes wait at the gate for those who wish to explore the island's lavender-scented trails. A wood-fired pizza oven anchors the outdoor terrace where evenings unfold slowly.
